ℹ️ About

MemoryAI uses the Ebbinghaus Forgetting Curve with a personalized forgetting rate (λ) that adapts to YOUR actual recall performance.

The math: R(t) = e−λt/S
Where R = retention, t = days, λ = your forgetting rate, S = memory stability.

After each revision, λ updates: λnew = λold × (predicted / actual)
This makes predictions more accurate over time — your personal brain model.
